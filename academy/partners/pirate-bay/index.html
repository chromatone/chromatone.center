Academy / Partners / Pirate Bay

Pirate Bay
A non-profit place for the digital community. The goal is to unite digital related people in Phuket to share knowledge, work and have fun together.
https://www.piratebayphuket.com/
32, 198 Phoonpon Rd, Tambon Talat Nuea, Mueang Phuket District, Phuket, Thailand
Phuket
Thailand

On a tropical island of Phuket there's Pirate Bay - the new fablab space for makers of different crafts to meet and collaborate on creative projects. It is located in the middle of Phuket Town and hosts a number of regular meetups for local communities. Thanks Glenn Miller for making all this a reality.

It was almost a year of collaboration. 35 meetups is a huge experience we got there!

Events and activities will depend on a day time ​

Here examples of events you can visit in Pirate bay Phuket:

Morning – education for kids, examples: ​

  • 3D modeling in Tinkercad for kids
  • Making a custom toy with a 3D printer
  • Create your own game in Roblox
  • Cryptocurrencies explained for kids

Daytime ​

  • Coworking and cafe
  • Members doing their projects

Evening – Events for adults, for example: ​

  • 3D printing for home applications
  • How to use Smart-contracts in real life
  • Environment problems and tech solutions
  • Smart cannabis farming

Night time – Electronic music events ​
