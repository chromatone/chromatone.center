Jam
A visual guide for collaborative music events

Apps for improvisational performances and casual jams. You can use our Jam Session to display all the vital parameters of a session. Or put your device with Jam Table app on the screen in front of your synths and players. Or press the Random button in Random Jam app and play to a cleverly generated set of BPM, tonic note and a scale. We can add more for your need - feel free to contact us.

Random Jam
A simple randomizer for basic jam parameters - BPM, Tonic and Scale.
